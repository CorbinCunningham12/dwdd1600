Our Story

Before 2008, Angelo's family started a wheat farm just off the coast of new york on newly developed floating platous.
After many years of success, in 2015, a tsunami that just happened to have something to do with the chinese hit the artificially floating field,
and the family had to relocate to the pacific to rebuild their lives, today we still sell bread.
